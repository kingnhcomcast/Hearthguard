HearthGuard turns a simple campfire into a pocket of safety. In the Overworld, hostile mobs feel the heat and hesitate, keeping their distance instead of rushing you. Drop a campfire at a new base, during a mining stop, or while you are caught out at night, and the space around the flames becomes a calm buffer that gives you room to build, sort, and breathe.

You still have to be smart about placement and line of sight, but the mod shifts the rhythm of survival: lighting a campfire is no longer just for ambiance, it is a tactical choice. The result is a world that feels a little more grounded and a lot more manageable without removing the tension of danger.

HearthGuard supports adjustable ranges and speeds, per-mob allowlists or blocklists, and optional one-time fear drops for a small reward the first time a mob is driven off.
